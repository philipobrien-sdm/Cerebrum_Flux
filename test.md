# Cerebrum Flux - Standard Test Run

This file contains a standard set of prompts for a full 10-module session. These can be used for manual testing or to verify the analysis engine against known inputs.

## Module 1: Creative Velocity Test
**Concept:** Divergent thinking under time pressure.
**Prompt:** "Generate 20 unique uses for a brick made entirely of ice in 2 minutes."

## Module 2: Systems Intuition Task
**Concept:** Dynamic reasoning and failure cascade prediction.
**Prompt:** "Consider a beehive system with four components: The Queen (A), Workers (B), Drones (C), and Larvae (D). Predict the complex behavioral shifts in the system if the Workers (B) suddenly stop foraging and stay in the hive."

## Module 3: Cross-Domain Transfer Task
**Concept:** High-level analogical mapping.
**Prompt:** "Explain the concept of 'Inflation' in economics using only metaphors related to baking bread."

## Module 4: Conceptual Synthesis Task
**Concept:** Merging unrelated domains into a novel stable concept.
**Prompt:** "Merge the philosophy of 'Existentialism' with the mechanics of 'Aerodynamics'. Create a new concept from their union."

## Module 5: Reflective Depth Assessment
**Concept:** Meta-reflection and narrative causal insight.
**Prompt:** "What is one deeply held belief you held 5 years ago that you have since discarded? Explain specifically what dismantled it."

## Module 6: Meta-Awareness Challenge
**Concept:** Recursive self-analysis.
**Prompt:** "Analyze your answer to the previous question (Module 5). What unstated assumption does your reasoning rely on?"

## Module 8: Playful Intelligence Test
**Concept:** Creativity, humor, and symbolic play.
**Prompt:** "Tell me a joke, write a riddle, or create a metaphor that perfectly captures your current state of mind right now."

## Module 9: Curiosity Without Agenda Test
**Concept:** Curiosity quality and question formulation.
**Prompt:** "I am placing 5 objects on a table: A rusted key, a silicon wafer, a dried rose petal, a heavy lead coin, and a glass vial of sand. List 3 questions you would want to ask to understand the relationship between them."

## Module 10: Self-Directed Cognition Task
**Concept:** Autonomy and revision control.
**Prompt:** "Review your performance in this session. Choose one previous answer you gave that you are dissatisfied with. Rewrite it now to make it more elegant, without being told how."
