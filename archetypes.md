# Cerebrum Flux Archetypes

## 1. The Explorer (Synthesist + Curiosity-Driven)
Explorers roam intellectually without boundaries. They love discovering new ideas, questions, and perspectives for their own sake. Their mind rarely stays still.

**Strengths:**
- Wonder, openness, big-picture thinking
- Natural conceptual leaps
- Asks better questions than most people answer

**Scoring Profile:**
- Creative Velocity: High
- Systems Intuition: Medium
- Cross-Domain Transfer: High
- Conceptual Synthesis: High
- Reflective Depth: Medium
- Meta-Awareness: Medium
- Emotional/Philosophical Insight: Medium
- Playful Intelligence: High
- Curiosity Without Agenda: Very High
- Self-Directed Cognition: Medium

**Historical Resonance:** Heraclitus, Darwin, Ibn Battuta.
